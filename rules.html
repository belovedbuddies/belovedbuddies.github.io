Welcome to VirtueVibes

At VirtueVibes, we prioritize creating a positive and respectful environment for all members. Below are the rules and guidelines that govern our community:

Community Guidelines

  • Kindness and Respect: Treat others with kindness and respect at all times.
  • Positive Communication: Engage in constructive and positive discussions.
  • Privacy: Respect others' privacy and confidentiality.
  • Content Policy: Avoid sharing offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
  • Abhaya Tool: Utilize the Abhaya tool for resolving conflicts peacefully and maintaining harmony.

Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans depending on the severity and recurrence of the offense.
